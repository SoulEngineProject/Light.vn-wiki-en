## Variable Operations

- `+`: add, positive
- `-`: subtract, negative
- `*`: multiply
- `/`: divide
- `%`: modulus
- `<`, `<=`
- `>`, `>=`
- `**`: power
- `~`: bit not
- `|`: bit or
- `&`: bit and
- `||`: or
- `&&`: and
- `<<`: bit left shift
- `>>`: bit right shift
- `==`: equal
- `!=`: not equal
